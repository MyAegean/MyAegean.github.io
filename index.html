What is MyAegean?

"MyAegean" is a non-profit voluntary initiative by students of the University of the Aegean. Also, a community portal, e-magazine & forum.

Our aspiration is to create a vehicle which will encourage and promote direct, collective and qualitative communication and information exchange, without geographical restrictions, among all members of the University of the Aegean`s community and the local or broader communities and people who like to participate.
